Chicken Enchiladas

Description:Chicken enchilada with cream of chicken soup, spicy chicken and chilie peppers are rolled up in flour tortillas and baked with cheese on top.

Ingredients:

  • 1 10.5 ounce can of condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 cups chopped chicken breast
  • 1 4 ounce can chopped green chile peppers, drained
  • 8 8 inch tortillas
  • 1 cup shredded cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Mix soup and sour cream in a small bowl
  3. Melt butter a saucepan over medium-high heat. Add onion and chili powder, saute until tender,
  4. Stir in chicken, chile peppers, and 2 tablespoons of the soup mixture.
  5. Spread 1/2 cup of the soup mixture in baking dish. Take about 1/4 cup of chicken mixture down center of each tortilla.
  6. Roll up tortilla and place them seam side down in baking dish. Spread rest of mixture of top of tortillas.
  7. Bake in oven for 25 minutes.
