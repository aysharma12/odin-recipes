Garlic Noodles

Description: This slow cooker recipe simmers chicken to a sweet and savory perfection. Tossed in with some warm, springy noodles, and you have a great meal.

Ingredients:

  • 1/3 cup honey
  • 1/4 cup low sodium soy sauce
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 1 tbsp shaoxing wine
  • 2 tsp toasted seasme oil
  • 2 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 pound boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 2 tbsp water
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch
  • 16 ounces lo mein noodles, cooked
  • 3 scallions, thinly sliced
  • white seasme seeds

Steps

  1. Mix honey, soy sauce, garlic. dark soy sauce, oyster sauce, sriracha, shaoxing wine, ginger, seasme oil, and salt in slow cooker. Cook on high for about 4 hours.
  2. Take chicken out to cut up, stir in cornstarch and water slurry into put for 30 seconds. Then place chicken, cooked noodles, and scallions, and toss to coat.
