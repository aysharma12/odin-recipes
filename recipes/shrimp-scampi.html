Shrimp Scampi

Description: Butter, garlic, and white wine sauce shrimp that you can cook as an appetizer or served with noodles to make it a complete dish.

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound large shrimp
  • 16 ounce linguine
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 2 finely diced shallots
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1/2 cup dry white wine
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • salt & pepper
  • a pinch of red pepper flakes
  • 1/2 cup parsley

Steps:

  1. Bring large pot of salted water to boil and add pasta and cook until nearly done.
  2. Melt butter and stir in oil, shallots, garlic, and red pepper flakes until shallots are translucent. 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Season shrimp with salt and pepper; add to skillet and cook until pink, stirring occasionally, 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Set aside shrimp and add white wine and lemon juice to skillet and bring to boil. Scrapping the bottom of pan meanwhile.
  5. Add 2 more tablespoons of butter and oil and bring to simmer.
  6. Toss in pasta, shrimp, parsley, salt, pepper, and a little oil.
  7. Ready to eat with a side of garlic bread
